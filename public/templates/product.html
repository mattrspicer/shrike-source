INSTALL IN 4 EASY STEPS

1. Remove the passenger foot peg.
2. Install the Shrike Mounts peg and secure with pin.
3. Adjust and tighten the jack screws.
4. Mount the receiver with the plate and bag attached.

ABOUT THE SYSTEM
THE PEG

—

Easy to install and looks like a
stock foot peg.
THE JACK SCREWS

—

Holds the peg firmly in place. Surprisingly rigid!
THE RECEIVER

—

Mates perfectly to the peg providing a solid mount for your stuff!
